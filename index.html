Sofie Steegen is what may be called a multifaceted artist., passionate about pottery and drawing. she invests a lot of her time and energy into meeting new people who will trigger a unique, moving inspiration for her work. As a potter, she revises materials’ purpose and spontaneously combines elements to create uniquely designed pieces. There’s nothing like positive energy to keep her going through her projects; because things aren’t always easy and can take some time, patience is a really important quality. Sofie captures the essence of craftsmanship and stays close to the creating process of its carefully handmade products.
